Frequenty Asked Questions

  • How do we know that a product is real or fake?

    An authentic product will have a unique code that will be used to verify the authenticity of the product. If the product is fake, it will not have a unique code and will be rejected by our system.

  • What is the diffrence between a first copy and a fake priduct?

    A first copy is a product that is made by a company that is not the original manufacturer of the product. A fake product is a product that is made by a company that is not the original manufacturer of the product and is made to look like the original product.

  • What to do if I receive a fake product?

    If you receive a fake product, you can contact us and we will make sure that you get a refund.

  • What methods do you use to ensure the authenticity of the product?

    We use a unique code that is generated for every product. This code is used to verify the authenticity of the product. If the product is fake it will be rejected by our system.
